    How to play Kobold Cave

    Kobold Cave is a simple game where you buy Kobolds in an effort to make a platinum coin to wake the dragon! Kobolds can be used to craft coins, trades coins from the outside, make equipment, and use that equipment to fight monsters in the dungeons!

    Every kobold has a two basic stats: Energy (), and Hunger (). Go to the den to rest, and go to the cooking energy to fill up! Kobolds will automatically go to these locations when they run out of hunger or energy.

    Kobolds gain experience when they do things. The four kinds of Experience are:

    • : General Experience

      • This is gained through just existing and cooking!

    • : Crafting Experience

      • This is gained through crafting coins or smithing equipment!

    • : Trading Experience

      • This is gained by trading coins in the outside world!

    • : Adventuring Experience

      • This is gained by fighting monsters in the dungeon!

    Kobolds can also adventure in the Dungeon. They require a weapon and an armor though, and will run when a piece breaks. Move them to the entrance to give them equipment if there are any to take, and they'll automatically go into the dungeon to fight! Defeating creatures in the dungeon yields valuable gems () to help speed up your progress toward waking the dragon!
